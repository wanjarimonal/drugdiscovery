The AI Opportunity

Artificial intelligence

AI is being used, since it is capable of handling large volumes of data with enhanced automation. Many drug molecules are developed due to the vast chemical space, which contains more than 1e+60 molecules. Due to a lack of advanced technologies, drug development is a time-consuming and expensive process, which can be addressed by using artificial intelligence. With the help of artificial intelligence, drug targets can be validated and drug structure designs can be optimized more quickly
